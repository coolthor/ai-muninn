## Plain-Language Version: What are AI assistants?

AI assistants (like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) are software you can chat with by typing. You ask questions, and they answer in text — they can translate, write emails, organize notes, or explain things you don't understand. According to a [Pew Research Center survey (2024)](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2024/03/26/americans-use-of-chatgpt-is-ticking-up-but-few-trust-its-election-information/), 23% of American adults have used ChatGPT, and global monthly active users for AI assistants exceeded 500 million by 2026. You don't need to know how to code. If you can type, you can use them. This article introduces the three most popular AI assistants and helps you pick one.

## You've heard of "AI" but don't know where to start

Everyone's talking about AI. It's all over the news. You feel like you should try it — but when you search "how to use AI," you get a wall of tool names, pricing plans, and jargon you don't understand.

It's not that complicated.

In 2026, there are really just three AI assistants you need to know about. They all work the same way: you type a question, and the AI types back an answer. Like texting a friend who knows everything. The difference is that each of these three "friends" has a different personality.

This article introduces all three so you can pick one and get started.

## ChatGPT — The one everyone knows

![ChatGPT landing page](/images/ai-ask-right/chatgpt-landing-en.png)

**Made by:** OpenAI, an American AI company.

**In one sentence:** The world's most popular AI chatbot. Ask it anything — write an email, translate text, brainstorm ideas, generate images, write code. It tries to do everything.

### What can you do for free?

Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) — you can start chatting without even signing up. The free tier gives you about 10 messages every 5 hours. After that, it switches to a weaker model (answers get less detailed).

Features NOT available for free: image generation, video generation, deep research, memory (it won't remember your previous conversations).

### What if you pay?

| Plan | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic chat, ~10 messages per 5 hours |
| **Plus** | $20/month | More messages, image generation, deep research, memory |
| **Pro** | $200/month | Nearly unlimited usage, strongest model, video generation |

Most people are fine with the free tier. If you're using it daily and hitting limits, consider Plus. Pro is for power users.

### What's it best at?

- Jack of all trades — the most "do everything" option
- Image generation (describe a scene, it draws it)
- Web search (can look up current information, not just memory)
- Good Chinese language support

### How to start

Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and type in the input box. To save your chat history, click "Sign up free" in the top right. Mobile app available on App Store and Google Play — search "ChatGPT."

## Claude — The best writer and analyst

![Claude landing page](/images/ai-ask-right/claude-landing-en.png)

**Made by:** Anthropic, another American AI company (many of its founders came from OpenAI).

**In one sentence:** An AI that's especially good at reading and writing. Hand it a long document, ask it to summarize, rewrite, or analyze — it does this better than the others.

### What can you do for free?

You need to sign up first (unlike ChatGPT and Gemini). Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and register with Google or email.

The free tier gives you about 5-20 messages per day (longer messages cost more quota). You can upload images and documents for analysis.

![Claude chat interface](/images/ai-ask-right/claude-chat.png)

### What if you pay?

| Plan | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic chat, ~5-20 messages per day |
| **Pro** | $20/month | 5x more messages, project management, Google integration, memory |
| **Max** | $100-200/month | Strongest model, more quota, faster responses |

### What's it best at?

- Long document analysis (give it a 50-page PDF and ask for a summary — it won't miss things)
- Writing quality (given the same topic, Claude's output tends to be more natural and well-structured)
- Translation (in blind tests, Claude's translations beat Google Translate and DeepL)
- Excellent Chinese support, especially Traditional Chinese

### How to start

Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai), click "Continue with Google" or enter your email. Mobile app available — search "Claude" on App Store or Google Play.

## Gemini — Google's AI

![Gemini landing page](/images/ai-ask-right/gemini-landing-en.png)

**Made by:** Google.

**In one sentence:** An AI that's wired into everything Google. If you already use Gmail, Google Docs, and Google Calendar, Gemini can work with all of them directly.

### What can you do for free?

Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) — if you have a Google account (you probably do), you can start right away. The free tier gives you 50 daily credits, which is plenty for casual use.

### What if you pay?

| Plan | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic chat, 50 daily credits |
| **AI Plus** | $7.99/month | More credits, more storage |
| **AI Pro** | $19.99/month | Strongest model, deep research, video generation |

Gemini's paid plans are cheaper than the other two, and Google frequently runs promotions (like half-price for the first two months).

### What's it best at?

- Google integration ("Turn this Gmail into a to-do list." "Draft meeting notes in Google Docs.")
- Real-time search (it IS Google — looking things up is literally its core business)
- Multilingual (Chinese, English, even Cantonese)
- If you're on Android, Gemini can be your phone's built-in assistant

### How to start

Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) and sign in with your Google account. Mobile app available — search "Gemini" on App Store or Google Play.

## Not sure which to pick?

Don't overthink it.

**Just want to try it out** — Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com). No sign-up required. Just type and see what happens. Lowest barrier to entry.

**Already have a Google account** — Try [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com). Your Gmail and docs are already there.

**Need help with documents or writing** — Sign up at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai). Claude is the most consistent at this.

All three are free. Trying one and not liking it costs you nothing. The best approach is to ask the same question to all three and see which answer you like best.
